Nail Design & Techniques

There are a variety of polishes that one can choose from, that have different finishes. Each has pros and cons and can be used for different styles or art.

  • Creme Polish

    • A creme is one of the most popular finishes; it's what you classically imagine when you think of manicures. This is a plain, smooth color. No shimmer, no glitter, no muss, no fuss.

  • Jelly Polish

    • A jelly has less opacity than cremes. They can be extremely glossy and are great for layering; for gradients and for "sandwiches."

  • Holographic Polish

    • Holographic polishes contain very fine, rainbow colored glitter, which changes color on the reflection of light. This polish is very dazzling in direct sunlight.

  • Glitter & Shimmer Polishes

    • Glitter is one of most glamourous finishes. They may have fine or macro glitter of varrying shapes and sizes.

  • Others

    • There are all sorts of fun polishes. I have several that glow in the dark, are blacklight reactive, change with UV exposure, or are thermal reactive. Branch out and have fun!

Routine/Care

After taking off polish, it can be important to hydrate the cuticle or do some maintenance. I currently hydrate with Spinster Sisters lotion and temple rub.

Favorite Brands
